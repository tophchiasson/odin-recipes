Guacamole

Guacamole is one of mankind's greatest achievements. A sultry collection of avocados, onions, spices, and elbow grease, it's sure to please both the young, and the young at heart.

Where this recipe lacks in complexity, it makes up for with avocados.

Ingredients

  • Avocados
  • Salt
  • Elbow Grease

Steps

  1. Cut up those avocados
  2. Mash em up, til they're all smooth and stop asking questions
  3. Salt Salt Salt
  4. Pepper Pepper Pepper
  5. Tomatoes if you're feelin' nasty
